Scared Of Heights




Scared of Heights? Well, overcome it; because in this hyper-casual game you need to race to the finish line while flipping above high mountains and terrains as well as dodging trees, rocks and anything nature throws at you!

  • I worked as a game programmer (in a team of 2 including me).
  • Build gameplay mechanics (for 1st prototype). Worked on 2 different iterations/ prototypes for this concept.
  • Worked on random obstacles genrator with customisable difficulty according to the level number.
  • Done A/B testing to analyze user data to monitor different mechanics for retention.
  • Integrated SDKs for game analytics, notification, and advertisement mediation.
