Gorilla Chase




With over half a million downloads and consistently in the Top 50 (Action Category in US), Gorilla Chase is a game of tag with a twist where either you need to survive angry lava gorillas or tag as many as you can!

  • I worked as a game programmer and game designer (in a team of 4 including me).
  • Built all the gameplay architechture and helped in the UI scripting. Also, made all the UI art works.
  • Worked on costume shop, tag and collectables scripting. And worked on game optimisation and smoothness.
  • Analyzed data from users to monitor response and satisfaction rates. Increase retention from 22% to 38% and decreased CPI from 0.67$ and 0.44$ to 0.18$ and 0.12$ on iOS and android respectively.
